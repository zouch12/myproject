ABOUT

DCYO is one of the first football clubs in the village of Dennery. The club carries the reputation of being very strick with discipline. This factor is due to the high number of players and other member that part of the police and fireservice departments in Saint Lucia.

With the reputation that preceeds the club, is also the smooth and careful nature of playing. They are known to execute strategies fluently against their rivals and are quite accomplished in the championship category.
